Wed, 22 Nov 2023 08:00:00 GMT

Hyderabad: Nacharam police seized Rs 1.20 crore unaccounted money. During vehicle checking, the police team intercepted a white Hyundai car (TS 08 FY8796) at Nacharam crossroads.

The car was heading from Habshiguda towards Bhongir district. The driver and another person in the car were taken into custody for interrogation.

The Returning Officer (RO) was also present.

The seized cash will be handed over to the EC and IT departments, if the relevant documents are not produced, a police officer said.
