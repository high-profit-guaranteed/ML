Tesla (TSLA) is offering more value yet its share price is falling. Hence, some people will wonder if Elon Musk’s electric vehicle maker could become a value investment?

Tesla’s share price fell from $175.66 on 20 March 2023 to $172.03 on 20 March 2024. Conversely, Tesla’s total assets grew by 29.49% from $82.34 billion in December 2022 to $106.62 billion in December 2023. However, Tesla’s total liabilities grew by 18.03% from $36.44 billion in December 2022 to $43.01 billion in December 2023.
