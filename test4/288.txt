Cathie Wood-led ARK Investment Management on Monday sold a whopping stake in Tesla Inc TSLA possibly booking some lucrative profits after the stock surged 131.11% since the beginning of 2023.

Funds operated by ARK sold over 393,000 shares of the EV-maker at an estimated valuation of over $98 million based on Monday's closing price. The sale was done via the flagship ARK Innovation ETF ARKK, ARK Autonomous Technology & Robotics ETF ARKQ and the ARK Next Generation Internet ETF ARKW.
