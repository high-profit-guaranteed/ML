BATON ROUGE, La. (WAFB) - Looking for a new job? Amazon is hiring for its new facility at the old Cortana Mall site in Baton Rouge.

Mayor-President Sharon Weston Broome announced Amazon will begin posting jobs for the over 1,000 full-time positions at the new facility in Baton Rouge on Friday evening, May 17. The positions will reportedly be posted on a rolling basis for the next several weeks.

Positions include warehouse jobs, delivery drivers, fulfillment center workers, store associates, and many more hourly positions.
