Does Tesla have the right business model for autonomous driving?

Tesla (TSLA -4.90%) is improving FSD rapidly, and users are raving about version 12 of the software, which is built from the ground up with artificial intelligence. But Tesla may have a big problem on its hands.

In this video, Travis Hoium shows how Tesla's business model may not be built well for autonomous driving as competitors scale a completely different, more disruptive model.
